Fitness Tools

Our fitness tools are designed to make it easy to calculate calorie requiements, macros, body fat percentage and strength goals.

Bench Press Calculator: Calculate Your 1 REP MAX (1RM)

The Muscle & Strength bench press calculator can be used to work out your approximate 1 REP MAX bench press. These figures are not 100% accurate, but it serves as a good guide!

BMR Calculator: Calculate Your Basal Metabolic Rate & Calorie Requirements

The basal metabolic rate (BMR) and calorie calculator is an excellent tool for estimating how many calories your body needs on a daily basis depending on the amount and intensity of your exercise regime.
