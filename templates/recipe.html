The Bounce-Back Protein Smoothie Recipe

Written By : Hanma Baki

Published on : 2024-06-01

Plan on hitting the town hard this weekend? Drink the Bounce Back Smoothie the morning after to recover & have a solid training session the very next day!

PREP TIME

~ 5 mins

COOK TIME

~ 1 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 1/2 cup of frozen berries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup of spinach
  • 1/2 cup of oats
  • 1 tbsp of chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp of honey

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 400
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 6g

Cooking Instructions

Add all ingredients into a blender Mix for 30 seconds
